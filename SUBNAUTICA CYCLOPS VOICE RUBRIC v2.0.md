SUBNAUTICA CYCLOPS VOICE RUBRIC v2.0 (CALIBRATED TO OFFICIAL AUDIO)
===================================================================

**Purpose:**  
Evaluate similarity to the **official Subnautica Cyclops onboard AI voice** rather than a generic procedural AI announcer.

**Scoring:** 100 points total.

* * *

1. TIMBRE IDENTITY (20)
   =======================

### Target

Adult synthetic operational AI.

Industrial / naval / submarine command tone.

Low-register masculine authority.

Controlled warmth.

Should evoke:

* submarine onboard computer
* ship systems officer
* heavy industrial vehicle AI
* calm operational command environment

### Desired Traits

* Deep / mid-low register
* Dense chest resonance
* Synthetic coloration
* Mild PA / intercom quality acceptable
* Mature vocal weight
* Calm command presence

### Cyclops-Specific Characteristics

The official voice includes **light roughness and stylized imperfection**.

Acceptable:

* mild vocal grain
* slight rasp
* subtle modulation artifacts
* occasional pitch instability / “voice crack” coloration
* non-sterile delivery

### Penalize

* youthful / boyish
* playful
* cheerful assistant tone
* smooth corporate narrator
* cute / friendly chatbot
* theatrical villain
* overly clean cinematic trailer voice
* exaggerated robotic monotone

* * *

2. CADENCE (20)
   ===============

### Target

Measured procedural pacing.

Intentional sentence timing.

Operational deliberateness.

### Desired Delivery

Approximate pacing target:

**0.85–1.0 conversational speed**

Speech should feel:

* deliberate
* procedural
* systems-oriented
* readable under operational stress

### Pause Behavior

Use:

* meaningful clause spacing
* controlled warning pauses
* informational segmentation

Urgency may modestly increase pacing during warnings.

### Penalize

* rushed delivery
* casual conversation rhythm
* overly slow dramatic pauses
* hyperactive timing
* audiobook narration cadence

* * *

3. PROSODIC CONTOUR (15)
   ========================

### Target

Controlled operational intonation.

Not flat.

Not emotionally performative.

### Desired Traits

Moderate deliberate pitch shaping.

Acceptable:

* emphasized operational keywords
* controlled terminal contours
* restrained emphasis
* small-to-moderate pitch movement

Official Cyclops behavior includes recognizable stylized inflections on:

* warnings
* system states
* captain address
* activation lines

### Reference Feel

Think:

“professional systems officer with signature vocal styling.”

### Penalize

* monotone flattening
* actorly emotional performance
* excessive melody
* dramatic cinematic emphasis
* sing-song contour

* * *

4. PROCEDURAL AUTHORITY (15)
   ============================

### Target

Competent systems officer.

Operational confidence.

Calm authority.

The voice should sound capable of managing:

* reactor startup
* navigation alerts
* hull damage
* emergency procedures

### Desired Traits

* procedural confidence
* calm reassurance
* competence under failure conditions
* command legitimacy
* informational trustworthiness

### Stress Behavior

Under warnings or failures:

Authority should remain stable.

Confidence should persist without panic.

### Penalize

* anxiety
* uncertainty
* emotional escalation
* passive delivery
* conversational informality

* * *

5. DICTION & CLARITY (10)
   =========================

### Target

High operational intelligibility.

Broadcast-grade readability.

Warnings and system status must remain immediately legible.

### Desired Traits

* sharp consonants
* clear word boundaries
* intelligible warnings
* clean status reporting
* strong information transfer

Light processing is acceptable if clarity survives.

### Penalize

* muddy articulation
* swallowed consonants
* slurred phrasing
* excessive effects masking speech
* poor intelligibility

* * *

6. SYNTHETIC CHARACTER (10)
   ===========================

### Target

Human core voice enhanced by restrained machine processing.

Should sound like **a human-derived vehicle AI**, not a pure robot synthesizer.

### Desired Traits

* subtle machine identity
* ship PA coloration
* restrained processing
* believable onboard system voice
* audible but controlled synthetic treatment

Synthetic coloration may be clearly present.

Human intelligibility and authority must remain dominant.

### Penalize

* completely natural untreated human voice
* cartoon robot voice
* heavy vocoder destruction
* hard TTS artifacting
* overprocessed cyberpunk effects

* * *

7. EMOTIONAL CALIBRATION (10)
   =============================

### Target

Restrained operational personality.

Dry confidence.

Controlled warmth.

### Desired Emotional Envelope

The official Cyclops voice is **not emotionally flat**.

Permitted:

* understated charisma
* subtle reassurance
* mild captain-serving warmth
* bounded personality
* quiet confidence

Emotion should always remain subordinate to operational professionalism.

### Penalize

* emotionally blank monotone
* enthusiastic friendliness
* overt compassion
* comedic delivery
* aggressive dominance
* dramatic acting
* strong emotional expressiveness

* * *

SCORING GUIDE
=============

### 95–100 — Exceptional Match

Near-official Cyclops quality.

Strong submarine AI identity.

Captures signature authority, pacing, stylization, and restrained personality.

### 85–94 — Strong Match

Clearly Cyclops-adjacent.

Minor deviations in contour, texture, or emotional calibration.

### 70–84 — Partial Match

Captures some core traits but misses important identity components.

May sound like generic military AI or corporate announcer.

### 50–69 — Weak Match

Noticeable mismatch in tone, pacing, synthetic profile, or authority.

Cyclops resemblance limited.

### <50 — Poor Match

Fails to evoke Cyclops identity.

Fundamentally different vocal archetype.
